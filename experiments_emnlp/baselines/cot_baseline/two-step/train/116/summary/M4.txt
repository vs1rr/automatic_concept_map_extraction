The Bramshill mansion in Britain, home to the prestigious Police College, is known for its many ghosts, including the White Lady who announces her presence with a floral fragrance. The mansion, built in the 17th century, has a history of tragic events and is said to be haunted by various spirits, such as the Grey Lady and the Green Man. Visitors and residents have reported encounters with these ghosts, making Bramshill known as the 'most haunted house in Hampshire'. Despite the eerie tales, most of the ghosts are considered friendly and not harmful. The College Secretariat keeps a record of ghost sightings, encouraging visitors to share their paranormal experiences for the "ghost file".

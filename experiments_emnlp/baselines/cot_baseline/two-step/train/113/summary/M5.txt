The Borobudur in Civilization V is a World Wonder that provides a significant Faith bonus and free Missionaries when built in a Holy City. This Buddhist temple in Java, built between 778 and 850 AD, features intricate carvings and statues, representing different realms of Buddhist cosmology. The structure was lost for centuries before being rediscovered in 1814 by Sir Stamford Raffles. The Borobudur is a key religious Wonder in the game, aiding in the spread of your religion without the need for specific Social Policies.

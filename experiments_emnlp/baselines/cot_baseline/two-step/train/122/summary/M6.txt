Bruce Castle Museum in Tottenham offers valuable resources for Family History and Local History projects, housing archives such as maps, census returns, newspapers, and historical photographs. The museum, located in a 16th Century manor house, also organizes exhibitions and workshops. Additionally, the museum collaborates with groups like the Friends of Bruce Castle and Haringey Local History Forum. The museum's support has been instrumental in the development of projects like the 'Summerhill Road' website. For those interested, the museum can be contacted for further information or to join their mailing list.

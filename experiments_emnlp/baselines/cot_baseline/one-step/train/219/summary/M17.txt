Episode 8 of "Manhattan" delves into the challenges faced by scientists in developing a plutonium bomb, particularly the issues with the Thin Man project due to impurities in the plutonium. The episode highlights the moral dilemmas faced by physicist Charlie Isaacs, who ultimately decides to pursue implosion as the solution. It also explores the fear of the Nazis obtaining the bomb first, drawing attention to the contributions of British scientists to the Manhattan Project. The role of women at Los Alamos and the struggles they faced in pursuing their professional interests are also depicted. As the plutonium groups work to design a functional bomb, the upcoming episode promises further developments in the story.

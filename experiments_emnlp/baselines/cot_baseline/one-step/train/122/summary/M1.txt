Bruce Castle Museum Wood Green Library will be closed on Sunday 17 April for the installation of customer service facilities, reopening on Monday 18 April. The museum offers free entry and various activities, exhibitions, and tours. Visitors can plan weddings in the Grade I listed building with 18th-century rooms and parkland. The museum provides disabled access and offers a range of services including archive and local history resources, collections, and rooms for hire. Visitors can reach the museum by bicycle, foot, train, underground, bus, or car, with parking available at the rear of the museum. Standard opening hours are Wednesday to Sunday from 1pm to 5pm.

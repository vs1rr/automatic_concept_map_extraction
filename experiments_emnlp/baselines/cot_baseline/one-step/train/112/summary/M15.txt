The Book of Kells, a masterpiece of western calligraphy and illuminated manuscript, is a significant artifact from medieval Europe. Produced around 800 CE in Scotland and Ireland, it contains the four Gospels in Latin with exquisite calligraphy, decoration, rare colors, and embellishments. A set of three notebooks pays tribute to this remarkable work, showcasing the artistry of the Book of Kells. Published by Thames & Hudson, known for high-quality books on various subjects including art, design, history, and children's titles.

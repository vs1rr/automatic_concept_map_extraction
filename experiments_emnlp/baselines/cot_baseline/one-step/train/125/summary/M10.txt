The text provides a detailed account of the Spring Meeting of the West Virginia Archives and History Commission held on September 27, 2012, at the Bavarian Inn in Shepherdstown, West Virginia. The meeting was chaired by Robert Conte and attended by various voting and ex officio members. Discussions included approval of meeting minutes, reports on Archives and History, Historic Preservation, and National Register nominations, as well as updates on ongoing projects and grants. The meeting also addressed the review and approval of several National Register nominations for historical sites in West Virginia. Additionally, there was a discussion on revising and filing the commission's by-laws as procedural rules. The meeting concluded with setting dates for future meetings and adjournment at 1:14 p.m.

Yoram Tsafrir was an Israeli archaeologist known for his research on Byzantine influence on ancient synagogues, demography of Palestine, and excavations at various sites.
He was a Professor Emeritus at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem and a member of the Israel Academy of Sciences and Humanities.
Tsafrir's work focused on the archaeology and history of Palestine during different periods, and he contributed to various publications on the subject.
He also played a significant role in the reconstruction of a scale model of ancient Jerusalem.

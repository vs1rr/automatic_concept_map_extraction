Forest School is an independent school located on the edge of Epping Forest in Walthamstow, NE London.
Originally founded in 1834 as a boys' school, it became coeducational in 1981.
The school has a rich history, with famous alumni such as Benjamin Disraeli and connections to William Morris.
The campus includes a variety of facilities such as a theatre, computer center, and sports complex.
The Chapel of St John the Baptist, built in 1857, is a Grade II listed building on the campus

Belton House in Lincolnshire is a premier wedding venue with stunning gardens, a magnificent house, and marquee receptions.
The house is licensed for ceremonies for up to 60 guests in the Marble Room, with post-ceremony drinks available on the North Terrace.
The gardens offer countless photo opportunities, including the Italian Garden, Dutch Garden, and Orangery.
Afternoon receptions can be held in a marquee for up to 250 guests, with options for dining, dancing, and celebrating.
The venue also offers a luxurious wedding package at Belton Woods Hotel, including spa treatments, golf, and a champagne breakfast.

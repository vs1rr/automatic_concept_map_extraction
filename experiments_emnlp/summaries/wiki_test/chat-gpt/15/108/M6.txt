Belton House in Lincolnshire is a historic stately home with a rich history dating back to the 17th century.
The house was built by the Brownlow family and features a unique 'double pile' design.
The exterior showcases Carolean architecture, while the interior boasts a variety of styles from different time periods.
The house is surrounded by formal gardens, an Orangery, and a 12th-century parish church.

King Edward VIII, born in 1894, was the eldest son of King George V and became King in 1936.
He abdicated the throne later that year in order to marry Mrs. Wallis Simpson, causing a constitutional crisis.
He was succeeded by his brother, George VI.
Edward and Wallis became the Duke and Duchess of Windsor but were ostracized by the royal family.
Edward's views on appeasement and meeting with Adolf Hitler led to him being sent to the Bahamas during World War II.

Trinity College in Dublin, Ireland, is the oldest university in the country and is synonymous with the University of Dublin.
The college was founded in 1592 and is known for housing the famous Book of Kells in its library.
The Book of Kells is an illuminated manuscript Gospel book created around 800 AD in a Columbian monastery in either Britain or Ireland.
It is considered a masterwork of Western calligraphy and Insular illumination, featuring intricate illustrations and ornamentation that combine Christian iconography with Celtic motifs.

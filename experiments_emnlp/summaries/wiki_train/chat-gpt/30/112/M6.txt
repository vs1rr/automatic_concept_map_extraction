The Book of Kells is a medieval illuminated manuscript containing the four Gospels in Latin, with readings from the Old Latin translation.
It includes prefaces, "canon tables", summaries of gospel narratives, and characterizations of the evangelists.
The book is written on vellum in a script known as "insular majuscule" and contains 340 folios.
It measures approximately 33 x 25 cm and was rebound in the 19th century.
The manuscript is part of the Manuscripts & Archives Research Library at Trinity College Dublin and is available

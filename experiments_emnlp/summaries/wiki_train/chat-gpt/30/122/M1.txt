Bruce Castle Museum Wood Green Library will be closed on Sunday 17 April for the installation of customer service facilities, reopening on Monday 18 April.
The museum offers free entry and is open Wednesday to Sunday from 1pm to 5pm, with the option for visiting groups to book at other times.
The museum features various events, exhibitions, and activities, as well as a schools program and wedding hire in its Grade I listed building.
The museum also houses collections related to Haringey, available for online search.
Accessibility features include same-level access to the ground floor, a stair-lift to the first floor, wheelchair-accessible toilets, and touch tours by appointment.
Visitors are encouraged to arrive by bicycle or on foot, with bicycle parking available near the museum.
Public transportation options include the mainline train to Bruce Grove Station, buses 243, 123, and 318, and the Underground Victoria Line to Seven Sisters or Piccadilly Line to Wood Green Station.
Parking is available at the rear of the museum on Church Lane, with free parking for visitors.
The museum's address is Lordship Lane Tottenham N17 6RT.

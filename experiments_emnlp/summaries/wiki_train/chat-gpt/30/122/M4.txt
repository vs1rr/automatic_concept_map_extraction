Bruce Castle Museum in London is a 16th-century manor house with a 4-star rating.
Visitors appreciate the historical artifacts and displays, ranging from Roman times to the present day.
The museum features exhibitions on local history, including the Broadwater Farm Estate, which highlights community unity.
While some visitors find the museum in need of updates and more quality exhibits, others enjoy the charming atmosphere and free admission.
The museum is surrounded by a park with beautiful trees and a children's playground, making it a great place for a family day out.
Overall, Bruce Castle Museum offers a unique look into the history of Haringey and Tottenham, with a focus on community stories and local heritage.

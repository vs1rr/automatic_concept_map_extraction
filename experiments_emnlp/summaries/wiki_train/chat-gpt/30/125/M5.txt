The Capon Bridge United Methodist Charge consists of Capon Bridge, Capon Chapel, Central, and North River Mills churches.
They have a food pantry and clothing closet open on Tuesdays at Amazing Grace Baptist church.
Donations of specific items are requested, and cash donations can be mailed to the church.
Bible study and worship services are held at different times and locations throughout the week.
Heather Rogers is now a Certified Lay Minister leading worship services at different churches.
Louise Link was honored for 50 years of service.
Jane Arnold participated in a mission trip to Kenya.
The church emphasizes doing good and helping others.
They have various groups and events, including United Methodist Women meetings and cookbooks for sale.
The church's vision is to be holy healthy living churches, and their mission is to create opportunities for spiritual life.
They also have connections with other local and not-so-local churches.
Prayer needs are listed in the church bulletin and on their Facebook page.

The Washington-Centerville Public Library is showcasing a rare facsimile edition of the Book of Kells, a renowned 9th-century illuminated manuscript.
The library will display one page of the book each week, allowing visitors to appreciate its beauty and history.
The original Book of Kells is displayed at Trinity College in Dublin and attracts half a million visitors annually.
The library hopes to educate the public about this remarkable book, considered one of the most beautiful ever written.

Bruce Castle Museum in London is a Grade I listed 16th-century manor house that has been a museum since 1906.
It houses the Borough of Haringey's local history collections, archives, and art exhibitions.
The museum holds regular historical and arts events and is set in 20 acres of parkland.
Admission is free, and the museum is open Wednesday to Sunday from 13:00 to 17:00.
The museum and archives are closed on certain holidays.
Visitors interested in viewing the archives are encouraged to book an appointment.

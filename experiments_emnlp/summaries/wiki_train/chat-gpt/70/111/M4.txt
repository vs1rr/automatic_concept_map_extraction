Bodiam Castle was built between 1385 and 1388 by Sir Edward Dalyngrigge of East Sussex.
The castle is located in a lush, green area in South East England and is known for its beautiful landscaping.
The exterior of the castle features a wide moat, access bridge, and quadrangle surrounded by luxurious rooms.
The interior includes a Great Hall for banquets, a kitchen, living quarters, and open fireplaces.
Defensive technology at Bodiam Castle includes crossbows, hoarding, merlons, crenellations, murder holes, and more.
The castle also has a basement for storage and dungeons, as well as a pantry for perishable foods.
The castle is staffed by servants including a blacksmith, cook, maids, and hand servants.
The lady of the castle praises its beauty and sturdiness, while the blacksmith and cook discuss their roles in the castle.
Bodiam Castle is currently up for sale to the highest bidder.

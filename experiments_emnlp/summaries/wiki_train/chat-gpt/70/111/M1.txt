Bodiam Castle is a 14th-century moated castle with a ruined interior that offers a glimpse of medieval splendor.
Visitors can enjoy medieval character talks, family fun events, and guided tours with costumed characters.
The castle also features herb gardens and opportunities for learning, weddings, and wildlife exploration.
In the spring, visitors can enjoy the castle in the sunshine and participate in activities such as Easter egg hunts and St. George's Day events.
The castle's tea room and coffee shop offer homemade food, and the gift shop provides unique shopping opportunities.
Bodiam Castle also offers learning opportunities for school groups and college students, as well as volunteering opportunities for those interested in getting involved.
The castle is home to a variety of wildlife, including bats in the gatehouse towers.
Overall, Bodiam Castle provides a memorable and educational experience for visitors of all ages.

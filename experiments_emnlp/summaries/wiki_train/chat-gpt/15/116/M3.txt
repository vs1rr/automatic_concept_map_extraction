City & Country, a heritage developer, has acquired Bramshill, a significant Jacobean Prodigy House in Britain.
The Grade I Listed mansion, built in the 17th Century, features state rooms, banquet halls, and chapels.
The property has a rich history, with connections to royalty and the Home Office.
City & Country plans to restore the mansion, which has been used for training and other purposes over the years.
The acquisition is seen as a positive step for preserving the heritage asset.

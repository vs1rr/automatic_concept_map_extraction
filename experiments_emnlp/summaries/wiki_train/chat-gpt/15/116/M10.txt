Bellmans Auctioneers will be selling some of the contents of Bramshill House, a historic mansion in Hampshire, at their May auction.
The collection includes unique pieces such as a massive elm and oak refectory table, oak triple sconce candelabras by Robert 'Mouseman' Thompson, and oak ceremonial armchairs commissioned for Queen Elizabeth II and Prince Phillip.
The auction will take place over four days and will feature items denoted with a police helmet image

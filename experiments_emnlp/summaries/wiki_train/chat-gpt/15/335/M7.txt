Artist Joe Hesketh delves into the horrors of the Pendle Witch Trials through his art, drawing inspiration from his childhood in Pendle.
He reflects on how being different, like the accused witches, can lead to judgment and injustice.
Hesketh's investigation into the trials aims to shed light on the true story of these misunderstood individuals, who were likely just trying to survive in a harsh world.
By walking the path of the accused witches, Hesketh gains a deeper understanding of their experiences and the impact of the trials on society today.
Through his art, he hopes to educate others about this dark chapter in history and challenge societal norms that reject those who are different.

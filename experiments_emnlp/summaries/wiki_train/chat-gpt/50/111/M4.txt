Bodiam Castle was built between 1385 and 1388 by Sir Edward Dalyngrigge of East Sussex.
The castle is located in a lush, green area and is surrounded by a moat.
The interior of the castle features a Great Hall, kitchen, living quarters, and open fireplaces.
The castle also boasts defensive technology such as crossbows, hoarding, and murder holes.
There is a basement for storage and dungeons, as well as a pantry for perishable foods.
The castle comes with servants including a blacksmith, cook, maids, and guards.
The lady of the castle praises the elegant bedrooms and bathing areas, while the blacksmith and cook highlight their specialties in metalwork and cooking.
Overall, Bodiam Castle is a magnificent and well-defended structure that is now up for sale to the highest bidder.

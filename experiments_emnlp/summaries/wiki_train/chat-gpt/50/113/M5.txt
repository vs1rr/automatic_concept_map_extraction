The Borobudur is a World Wonder in Civilization V, introduced in the Brave New World expansion.
It provides a +5 Faith bonus and must be built in a Holy City.
Additionally, it grants 3 free Missionaries.
The structure is based on the Buddhist candi in Magelang, Java, consisting of six square platforms topped by three circular platforms with 72 small stupas.
It is adorned with 2672 bas-relief carvings and 504 Buddhist statues, representing the three realms of Buddhist cosmology.
Construction was completed between 778 and 850 AD during the Sailendra dynasty.
The temple was lost for centuries until rediscovered by Sir Stamford Raffles in 1814.
The Borobudur is a significant religious site for Buddhists and serves as a place for pilgrimage.

The Convention of 1832 failed to bring reforms, leading to the Convention of 1833 which aimed for independent statehood.
The Battle of Gonzales in 1835 marked the start of the Revolution, leading to the Convention of 1836 where the Declaration of Independence was written.
The Battle of the Alamo and the Massacre at Goliad resulted in many Texian deaths, but the Battle of San Jacinto in 1836 led to Texas winning independence from Mexico.

Artist Joe Hesketh delves into the history of the Pendle Witch Trials through his art, drawing parallels between his childhood in Pendle and the accused witches. He aims to shed light on the injustice faced by these individuals, who were misunderstood healers rather than evil magicians. By immersing himself in the story through research and walking the path of the Trials, Hesketh seeks to truly understand and convey the experiences of those involved. Through his work, he challenges societal norms and encourages a deeper appreciation of the Pendle Witches' story, highlighting the enduring impact of these events on modern society.

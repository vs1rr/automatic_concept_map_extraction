A New York society girl, Miss Edna Hilton, inherits the ghostly legend of "The Mistletoe Bride" after marrying Captain Denzil Cope, heir of the ancient Cope family in England. The tragic tale of the lost bride locked in a chest for fifty years haunts Bramshill House, where the newlyweds reside. Visitors report eerie encounters with spectres, adding to the mystery surrounding the old house. The story of the bride's fate, intertwined with lavish wedding festivities and a heartbreaking discovery, captivates the imagination and has inspired poems and ballads for centuries. The chilling legend continues to unfold as the ghostly presence follows the couple to their new residence at Lovell Castle in Oxfordshire.

An extremely rare promissory note signed by Jim Bowie was the top seller at Heritage Auctions' Texana Grand Format Auction, fetching $62,500. The auction featured various historical documents related to Texas history, including items signed by key figures like Sam Houston, Davy Crockett, and William Barret Travis. The auction realized a total of $587,701, with notable sales such as a historic broadside by Sam Houston and a map of Texas from 1867. The event attracted nearly 400 bidders eager to own pieces of Texas history that influenced the state's creation.

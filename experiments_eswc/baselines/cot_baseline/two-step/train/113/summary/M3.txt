A visit to a monastery provided insight into Theravada Buddhism, with a monk explaining the rules and practices followed by the monks. The tour included statues representing stages of the Buddha's quest for Enlightenment. Meditation followed, with guidance from the monk. The day continued with a visit to Borobudur Temple, a Mahayana Buddhist temple from the 9th Century, showcasing different levels symbolizing stages to reach enlightenment. The temple's history and restoration project were also highlighted, offering a contrast to the serene monastery visit.

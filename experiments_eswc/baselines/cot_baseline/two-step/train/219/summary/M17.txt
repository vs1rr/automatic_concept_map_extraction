Episode 8 of "Manhattan" delves into the challenges faced by scientists in developing a plutonium bomb, particularly the issues with the Thin Man project. Physicist Charlie Isaacs discovers that the bomb won't work due to impurities in the plutonium. The episode highlights the moral dilemma faced by Charlie and the urgency to prevent the Nazis from obtaining the bomb. It also sheds light on the contributions of the British Mission to the Manhattan Project and the role of women at Los Alamos. As tensions rise, the upcoming episode promises further developments in bomb design and the potential for Liza Winter to contribute to the project.

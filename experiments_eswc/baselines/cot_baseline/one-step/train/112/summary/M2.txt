The Washington-Centerville Public Library proudly presents a rare facsimile edition of the Book of Kells, a renowned 9th-century illuminated manuscript. Delve into the history of this exquisite book, considered one of the most beautiful ever created, and visit the library weekly to admire its pages. Just like at Trinity College in Dublin, where half a million people view the original annually, one page of the Book of Kells will be showcased each week. Experience the beauty and history of this masterpiece at the library in Centerville, Ohio.

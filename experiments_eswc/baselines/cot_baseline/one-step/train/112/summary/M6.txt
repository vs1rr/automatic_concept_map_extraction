The Book of Kells, housed in the Manuscripts & Archives Research Library at Trinity College Dublin, is a medieval illuminated manuscript containing the four Gospels in Latin. Written on vellum in a script known as "insular majuscule," it includes various prefaces and Gospel passages. The manuscript, with 340 folios, has been digitally scanned for online discovery and identification purposes. The Book of Kells is a significant work of art and religious text, showcasing intricate Celtic and medieval ornamentation.

A New York society girl, Miss Edna Hilton, inherits the ghostly legend of "The Mistletoe Bride" after marrying Captain Denzil Cope, heir of the ancient Cope family in England. The tragic tale of the lost bride locked in a chest for fifty years haunts Bramshill House, where the newlywed couple resides. Visitors report eerie encounters with spectres, adding to the mystery surrounding the old English legend. The story unfolds with a lavish wedding feast, a bride's disappearance during a game of hide-and-seek, and a heartbreaking discovery fifty years later. The tale of the mistletoe bride captivates imaginations, inspiring poems and ballads for centuries. The chilling events at Bramshill House continue to intrigue and terrify those who dare to explore its haunted history.

Bruce Castle Museum in Tottenham offers valuable resources for Family History and Local History projects, housing archives such as maps, census returns, newspapers, and historical photographs. The museum, located in a 16th Century manor house, also organizes exhibitions and workshops. Additionally, the museum collaborates with groups like the Friends of Bruce Castle and the Haringey Local History Forum. The Tottenham Civic Society aims to educate the public about the area's geography, history, and architecture. The museum's staff has been instrumental in supporting projects like the 'Summerhill Road' website. Contact information for joining these groups or getting on the museum's mailing list is provided.

Bruce Castle Museum is a 16th-century manor house in London, operated as a local history museum by Haringey Council. The museum showcases social history, local landscape, and works by local artists. The castle has a rich history, including connections to the Bruce family and notable renovations over the centuries. The museum also features exhibits on the Post Office, Rowland Hill, and the Tottenham Hotspur Football Club. The museum is free to enter and offers a glimpse into the social history of the area through various collections and artworks.

The voices of the Pendle witches charged with murder in 1612 will be brought to life in Lancaster University's upcoming Public Lectures. The lecture will explore the testimonies recorded by Thomas Potts, shedding light on this historical event. Other lectures will cover topics such as Lancaster's scientific contributions, the NHS, and nuclear new build. The event, titled 'Beyond the Lancashire Witches: Writing and Freedom,' will take place on November 27 and is free to attend. For more information, visit www.lancaster.ac.uk/50 or call 01524 592809.

The upcoming Public Lectures at Lancaster University will feature the testimonies of 10 Pendle witches charged with murder in 1612.
The lecture, titled 'Beyond the Lancashire Witches: Writing and Freedom', will explore the recorded testimonies of the women accused of using witchcraft to kill ten people.
The event will include a dramatization of the testimonies filmed in the Witches' Tower where they were imprisoned.
In addition to this lecture, other topics covered in the series include Lancaster's contribution to science, the changing face of the NHS, and nuclear new build

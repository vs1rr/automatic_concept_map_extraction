The text discusses the unification of Upper and Lower Egypt, which marked the beginning of ancient Egyptian history.
Before the unification, the two kingdoms were ruled by separate kings.
The unification occurred around 2686 BCE when the king of Upper Egypt invaded and conquered Lower Egypt.
The pharaohs of the united Egypt wore a double crown symbolizing the merging of the two kingdoms.
The people of unified Egypt worshipped the pharaoh as an incarnation of the hawk-god Hor

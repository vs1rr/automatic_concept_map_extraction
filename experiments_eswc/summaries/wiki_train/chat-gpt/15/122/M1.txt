Bruce Castle Museum Wood Green Library will be closed on Sunday 17 April for the installation of customer service facilities, reopening on Monday 18 April.
The museum offers free admission and various activities, exhibitions, and tours.
It also provides facilities for weddings and has collections related to Haringey available online.
The museum has disabled access and offers large print information and touch tours by appointment.
Visitors can reach the museum by bicycle, foot, train, underground, or bus.
Parking is available at the rear of the museum.
The standard opening hours are Wednesday to Sunday from 1pm to 5pm

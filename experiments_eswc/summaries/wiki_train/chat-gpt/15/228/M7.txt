An extremely rare promissory note signed by Jim Bowie was sold for $62,500 at Heritage Auctions' Texana Grand Format Auction in Dallas.
The note was drafted during the Convention of 1833 in San Felipe de Austin.
The auction, which realized $587,701, featured various historical documents related to Texas history, including items signed by fallen heroes of the Alamo like Davy Crockett and William Barret Travis.

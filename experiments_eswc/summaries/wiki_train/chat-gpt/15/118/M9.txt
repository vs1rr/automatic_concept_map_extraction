Lady Anne Clifford was a determined woman who fought for 40 years to regain her inheritance and restore her family's castles and churches to their former glory.
She was the last of a long line of Lancastrians and left a legacy that is still celebrated today.
Lady Anne's efforts included restoring Brougham Castle, which had fallen into ruin until it was rescued by the Ministry of Works in 1928.

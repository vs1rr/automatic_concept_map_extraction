Bodiam Castle was built by Sir Edward Dalyngrigge between 1385 and 1388 to showcase his status.
Located in East Sussex, the castle is surrounded by lush greenery and a moat.
The interior features a Great Hall, living quarters, and fireplaces.
Defensive technology includes crossbows, hoarding, and murder holes.
The castle also has storage in the basement and a pantry for perishable foods.
Servants, including a blacksmith and cook, are dedicated to their roles.
The lady of the castle praises its beauty and defenses.
The blacksmith creates armor and weapons, while the cook prepares delicious meals.
The castle is now up for sale to the highest bidder, offering a luxurious and well-defended living space with a rich history.

This text describes a guided walk from London to Bodiam Castle in East Sussex, organized by English Country Walks.
The walk includes a visit to the castle, a traditional pub lunch, and return rail fare from London.
The itinerary includes exploring the countryside, visiting historical sites, and enjoying a meal at a riverside pub.
The walk starts at London Charing Cross station and ends in the evening.
Prices vary based on group size, with options for a half-day

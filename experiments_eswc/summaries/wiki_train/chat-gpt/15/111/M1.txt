Bodiam Castle, a 14th-century moated castle with a ruined interior, offers a glimpse of medieval splendor.
Visitors can enjoy medieval character talks, family events, and guided tours.
The castle also features herb gardens, wildlife, and bats.
Special events include Easter egg hunts, St. George's Day activities, and springtime talks.
The castle's tea room and shop offer homemade food and unique products.

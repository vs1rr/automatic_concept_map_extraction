Bruce Castle Museum in London is a Grade I listed 16th century manor house that has been a museum since 1906.
It houses the Borough of Haringey's local history collections, archives, and art exhibitions.
The museum holds regular historical and arts events and is open Wednesday to Sunday from 13:00 to 17:00.
Admission is free, but if you want to visit the archives, you need to book an appointment.

The text discusses the Anglo-American Colonization Gallery Constitutional Alcalde of the Jurisdiction of Austin held in San Felipe de Austin in 1832, where resolutions were adopted to be presented to the Mexican Congress.
One resolution called for organizing a militia and committees of vigilance, safety, and correspondence.
Although the resolutions were not presented to the Congress, some were still upheld.
Edward Burleson was confirmed as a lieutenant colonel in a provisional militia.
In 1833, the Convention of 1833 met in San Felipe de Austin and petitioned for the separation of Coahuila and Texas

The Book of Kells is a renowned illuminated manuscript from medieval Europe, named after the Abbey of Kells where it was held.
Produced around 800 CE in Scotland and Ireland, it contains the four Gospels in Latin with expert calligraphy and decoration.
This set of three notebooks pays homage to the artistry of the Book of Kells, featuring exquisite craftsmanship and celebrating Ireland's greatest national treasure.

William C. Nixon was born in 1789 and died in 1869.
He served in the War of 1812 and was a member of the Virginia House of Delegates.
He married Mary Ann Caudy and had several children.
Chapon Chapel, where the first burial was William's mother-in-law, was thought to be built on his land.
The church was affiliated with the Primitive Baptists.

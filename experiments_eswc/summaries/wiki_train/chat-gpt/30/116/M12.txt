MEP Marta Andreasen wants the European Police College (Cepol) near Hook to be shut down due to alleged misuse of EU funding.
The college has been accused of using EU money for personal expenses, such as furniture and mobile phones.
Andreasen believes that the college, which educates policemen, should be held to high ethical standards and that using EU funding for personal purposes undermines its credibility.

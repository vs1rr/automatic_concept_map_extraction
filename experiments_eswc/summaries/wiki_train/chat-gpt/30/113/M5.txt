Borobudur is a World Wonder in Civilization V, introduced in Brave New World.
It provides a +5 Faith bonus and must be built in a Holy City.
The structure, located in Java, consists of six square platforms topped by three circular platforms with 72 small stupas.
It is adorned with 2672 bas-relief carvings and 504 Buddhist statues, representing Buddhist cosmology.
Construction was completed between 778 and 850 AD.

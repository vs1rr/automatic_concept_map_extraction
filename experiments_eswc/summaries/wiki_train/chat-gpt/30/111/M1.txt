Bodiam Castle is a 14th-century moated castle with a ruined interior that offers a glimpse of medieval splendor.
Visitors can enjoy various activities such as Easter egg hunts, medieval character talks, and family fun events for St. George's Day.
The castle also features herb gardens, group visits, and learning opportunities for school groups.
Additionally, Bodiam Castle is a popular wedding venue with its majestic towers and wide moat.
Wildlife enthusiasts can explore the castle grounds and spot unusual inhabitants, including bats in the gatehouse towers.
The castle also offers volunteering opportunities for those interested in getting involved.
Visitors can enjoy food and shopping at the castle's tea room and shop, which offer home-made food and unique products.
Overall, Bodiam Castle provides a unique and educational experience for visitors of all ages.

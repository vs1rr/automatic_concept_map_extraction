This text describes a guided walk from London to Bodiam Castle in East Sussex, led by English Country Walks.
The walk starts at London Charing Cross station and includes a train ride to Robertsbridge, a visit to Bodiam Castle, a traditional pub lunch, and a return train journey to London.
The walk passes through the countryside, with highlights including the historic village of Robertsbridge, a tour of Bodiam Castle, and a stop at the Salehurst Halt pub.
The price for the walk varies depending on group size, with options for a full-day or half-day trip.
Reservations are required and can be made by contacting English Country Walks.
The walk provides a unique opportunity to explore the beautiful landscape of rural East Sussex and learn about the history of Bodiam Castle.

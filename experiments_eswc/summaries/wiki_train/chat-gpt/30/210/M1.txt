Egypt is a country with over 7,000 years of history and civilization, making it a fascinating destination for holidays.
The country's strategic location, with the Nile River running through it and the Mediterranean Sea and Red Sea on either side, offers a wide variety of beaches, resorts, and cities to explore.
Cairo, the capital and largest city in Africa, is a vibrant and multicultural city with a mix of Pharaonic, Islamic, and Coptic monuments.
Outside of Cairo, visitors can enjoy day trips to places like Fayoum to see oases, lakes, and waterfalls.
Tour Egypt offers customized packages to fit different needs and budgets, ensuring a memorable experience for all visitors.
Whether you're interested in history, culture, or simply relaxing on the beach, Egypt has something to offer for everyone.

The Late Period of Ancient Egypt saw a decline in power after the Third Intermediate Period and before the Persian conquests.
The Twenty-Sixth Dynasty, also known as the Saite Period, was followed by the First Persian Occupation during the Twenty-Seventh Dynasty.
The Twenty-Eighth Dynasty was led by a single king who rebelled against the Persians, while the Twenty-Ninth Dynasty ruled from Mendes.

The text discusses the history and significance of castles around the world, highlighting their military, social, and architectural importance.
Castles began appearing in Europe around 800-1000AD as a way for local lords and nobles to secure their power and control over territories.
With the development of more cohesive military systems, castles became strategically important for defending and launching attacks.
However, the invention of gunpowder-based weaponry led to the decline of their military significance.
Despite this, castles remained popular as status symbols, grand residences, and administrative centers.
Today, castles are popular tourist attractions and can be found in many countries.

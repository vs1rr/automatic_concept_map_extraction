Isambard Kingdom Brunel was a celebrated civil and mechanical engineer known for his daring and innovative engineering projects, particularly the Great Western Railway.
Born in 1806, Brunel worked on various projects including the Thames Tunnel and the Clifton Suspension Bridge.
His work on the Great Western Railway, including structures like the Wharncliffe Viaduct and Box Tunnel, showcased his engineering prowess.

Frances Griffiths and Elsie Wright claimed to have photographed fairies in Cottingley Village, England in 1917.
The photos gained attention from spiritualists and Sir Arthur Conan Doyle, who believed in the existence of fairies.
However, in 1983, Elsie admitted that the photos were a hoax.
She and Frances had used paper cutouts from a book and hatpins to create the illusion of fairies.

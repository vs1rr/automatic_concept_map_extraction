During the Australasian Antarctic Expedition of 1912 to 1913, explorer Sir Douglas Mawson and his team used a cut-down sledge made of hickory and American ash supplied by L. Hagen & Co.
The sledges were used to carry provisions and equipment for their exploration of the Commonwealth Bay region.
Mawson led a sledging party with Xavier Mertz and Lieutenant Belgrave Ninnis, but tragedy struck when Ninnis fell into a crevasse, taking most of their supplies and dogs with him.

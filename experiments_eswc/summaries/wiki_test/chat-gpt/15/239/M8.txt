Lieutenant Belgrave Edward Sutton Ninnis, a heroic explorer, died 100 years ago during Douglas Mawson's Antarctic expedition.
Ninnis fell through a crevasse, losing essential supplies and dogs.
Mawson and Xavier Mertz continued their journey, facing hardships and eventually Mertz's death.
Mawson struggled on, reaching the main base after the ship had departed.
Ninnis' initials can still be seen

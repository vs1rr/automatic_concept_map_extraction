Vice President Hamid Ansari visited Angkor Wat temple in Cambodia, the world's largest Hindu temple complex, after India addressed Cambodian concerns over a replica being built in Bihar.
The visit included stops at the Mekong-Ganga Textile museum and Ta Prohm temple.
India assured Cambodia that the construction of the replica temple would not harm their relationship and made changes to the design to satisfy Cambodian officials.
The Angkor Wat temple is a popular tourist destination and a UNESCO World Heritage site.

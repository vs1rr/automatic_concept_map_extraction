Belton House in Grantham, Lincs, is a classic English country house with formal gardens and a deer park.
The estate includes a restaurant, café, and play area.
In 2015, Belton remembers its history as a camp for soldiers during WWI.
Visitors can explore exhibitions and events throughout the year.
Admission is £14.50 for adults.
Visitors are coordinating carpooling on the website, with some offering lifts and others looking for rides.

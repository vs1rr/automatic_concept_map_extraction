Belton House in Lincolnshire is a National Trust property with a large adventure playground, formal gardens, an orangery, and a landscape park.
The playground has play equipment for all ages and is surrounded by beautiful scenery.
Visitors can also explore the maze, take a miniature steam train ride, and enjoy the various amenities on site such as a cafe, restaurant, gift shop, and plant shop.
The house itself is a stunning restoration with attics and basements open on special dates.
While admission can be pricey, visitors can easily spend a whole day exploring the grounds and attractions.
The property is well-maintained and offers a variety of activities for families to enjoy.

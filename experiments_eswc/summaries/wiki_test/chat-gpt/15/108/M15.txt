Time Team filmed a program at a Machine Gun Corps training camp in Belton Park, Lincolnshire, which will be broadcast on Remembrance Sunday.
The team felt a sense of reverence while filming, and the experience was described as total immersion, similar to life in the trenches during the Great War.
The pressure of producing a good film in just three days was a great leveller, creating lifelong friendships among the team members.
The text reflects on the breakdown of class barriers during the war, with examples of officers forming close friendships with their batmen.
The impact of the Great War on modern society is discussed, with the author suggesting that it may take a full century to fully understand its effects.
The text also includes photos from the filming at Belton Park, showcasing the challenges of archaeological excavation and the use of Ground Penetrating Radar.

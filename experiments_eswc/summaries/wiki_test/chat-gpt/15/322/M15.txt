The legal history of Indian nations' relations with the federal and local government is complex, with questions about tribal sovereignty being legislated every year.

The jurisdictional patchwork on Indian lands hinders law enforcement, as documented in the award-winning series "Lawless Lands" by THE DENVER POST.
